Places to visit

I want to visit a several places all over the world. So far, I have only visited few places that grabbed my attention. Although there are many places in and out of the country that I'd love to visit. Bale Mountains National Park is the place I'd like to visit the most. The prospect of exploring the pristine landscapes of the Harenna Forest and the expansive Sanetti Plateau is an enticing adventure. Beyond the scenic vistas, the park offers a unique opportunity to witness the endangered Ethiopian wolf in its native environment, adding an element of rarity and ecological significance to the experience. I have summarised list of places I want to visit in the following table.

Place Location Description
Bale Mountains National Park Southeastern Ethiopia Bale Mountains National Park in Ethiopia features stunning landscapes, including the Harenna Forest and Sanetti Plateau. Ideal for trekking and wildlife enthusiasts, it offers a rare chance to witness the endangered Ethiopian wolf in its natural habitat.
Lalibela Rock-Hewn Churches Northern Ethiopia Lalibela is known for its 11 medieval churches carved out of solid rock, each with unique architectural features and religious significance. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Simien Mountains Northern Ethiopia A stunning mountain range with deep valleys, high plateaus, and unique wildlife, including the Ethiopian wolf and Walia ibex. Popular for trekking and breathtaking landscapes.
Halala Kela Resort Southwestern Ethiopia Halala Kela is a luxury resort located in the southwestern Ethiopia, near the lake created by the Gilgel Gibe III dam
